About AutoMall

Your trusted partner in finding the perfect vehicle for your needs

Our Story

Founded in 2005, AutoMall began as a small family-owned dealership with a passion for connecting people with their dream cars. What started as a modest lot with a handful of vehicles has grown into one of the region's most trusted automotive retailers.

Over the years, we've maintained our commitment to transparency, integrity, and exceptional customer service while expanding our inventory to include a wide range of makes and models to suit every budget and lifestyle.

Our team of automotive experts brings decades of combined experience to help you navigate the car buying or selling process with confidence.

Our Approach

At AutoMall, we believe buying a car should be an exciting, stress-free experience. That's why we've developed a customer-first approach that prioritizes your needs and preferences above all else.

We take the time to understand your lifestyle, budget, and driving habits to recommend vehicles that truly fit your requirements. Our no-pressure sales environment ensures you can browse at your own pace with expert guidance available when you need it.

Every vehicle in our inventory undergoes a rigorous 150-point inspection process, so you can shop with confidence knowing you're getting a quality automobile.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Integrity

We believe in honest, transparent dealings with every customer. No hidden fees, no pressure tactics - just straightforward car buying.

Quality

Every vehicle meets our strict quality standards. Our thorough inspection process ensures you get a reliable car that lasts.

Customer Care

Your satisfaction is our top priority. From your first visit to years after your purchase, we're here to support you.
